CGIWrap - How to get a copy

The current version of CGIWrap is always available from the primary ftp site on ftp.cc.umr.edu as well as on numerous mirror sites. If you know of a mirror that is not listed here, please let me know and I will add it to the list. Also let me know if one of these sites is no longer mirroring CGIwrap.

Primary Sites

  • http://www.umr.edu/~cgiwrap/dist/
  • ftp.umr.edu:/pub/cgi/cgiwrap/
